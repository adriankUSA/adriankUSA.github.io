ITIS 3135 Website Evaluations

Good CRAP Website: Superlist

Superlist is an all-in-one workspace for your to-dos, lists, notes, and projects.

  • Contrast: Superlist uses bright red, blue, purple, and white that contrast the black-grey background while also complimenting it.
  • Repetition: The website repeats its color patterns, reuses fonts, alignments, and images/styles of images.
  • Alignment: The website uses mostly a centered alignment in about the middle third of the screen, only having it different for the hero section.
  • Proximity: Sections are grouped appropriately with very nice spacing.

Bad CRAP Website: Pacific Northwest X-Ray Inc.

Pacific Northwest X-Ray Inc. is a company that sells X-Ray products and apparel.

  • Contrast: Colors do not contrast well, black on dark blue makes it hard to see.
  • Repetition: Well, the website does repeat its color schemes and patterns and images, but the execution is poor.
  • Alignment: The alignment is okay, it is all centered at the very least.
  • Proximity: Sections do not seem grouped together because there is very little spacing and everything is aligned the same. It is hard to gather your eyes to a specific section with intent.
