Welcome to RadiateTPS

RadiateTPS is a browser-based adaptation of the open-source OpenTPS software, designed to make cancer treatment planning systems more accessible to researchers, students, and clinicians.

What You'll Find Here

  • An introduction to Treatment Planning Systems (TPS)
  • A walkthrough of my undergraduate research project with Dr. Yonghong Yan
  • A gallery of dose visualization and ROI-based planning techniques
  • Technical breakdowns and architecture used to port OpenTPS to the web
  • A contact form to reach out with questions or feedback
